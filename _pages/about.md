I earned a Bachelor of Management in Information Management and Information Systems (GPA: 3.94/5.0) with a double degree in Finance (GPA: 3.72/5.0) from South China Agricultural University, and am now a master's student in Information Science at Nanjing University's School of Information Management.

My research interests lie in user behavior mining, health information dissemination, and computational social science. I use computational and data-driven methods — including statistical modeling, text mining, and econometric analysis — to study user behavior patterns in information environments and examine their intersections with health communication and social computing.

- **B.M. in Information Management & Information Systems**, South China Agricultural University, 2020–2024
