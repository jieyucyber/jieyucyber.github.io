I earned a Bachelor of Management in Information Management and Information Systems from South China Agricultural University and am now a master's student in Information Science at Nanjing University's School of Information Management. My research interests lie in user behavior mining, health information dissemination, and computational social science. I use computational and data-driven methods to study user behavior patterns in information environments and examine their intersections with health communication and social computing.

## Education

- **M.Sc. in Information Science**, Nanjing University, 2026–present
- **B.M. in Information Management & Information Systems**, South China Agricultural University, 2020–2024
- **B.A. (Double Degree) in Finance**, South China Agricultural University, 2020–2024
