Grounded in Social Cognitive Theory and the eHealth Comprehensive Usage Model, this study systematically investigated the mechanisms influencing residents' eHealth literacy using CGSS2021 survey data.

**Key Contributions:**
- Employed stepwise nested regression analysis in Stata, finding that active learning ability and individual cognitive ability significantly and positively affect eHealth literacy
- Conducted mediation analysis using the stepwise causal method and Sobel test, validating the mediating role of active learning ability between cognitive ability and eHealth literacy
- Performed heterogeneity analysis across gender, household registration, and income dimensions, revealing differential impact mechanisms across population subgroups
