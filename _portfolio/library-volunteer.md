## Volunteer Service — Guangzhou Library Service Team, SCAU Volunteer Association

Participated in the Guangzhou Library volunteer service team through the South China Agricultural University Volunteer Association.

- Organized and trained student volunteers for regular library service duties at Guangzhou Library
- Assisted with book sorting, shelving, and reader assistance services
- Contributed to children's science popularization activities and wrote content for the team's WeChat public account
